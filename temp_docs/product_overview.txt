Vendor Proof  —  S$149  (one-time, lifetime)The ProblemSingapore has tens of thousands of companies competing for government and enterprise procurement contracts. Buyers using Booppa can filter search results to show only verified vendors. If a vendor has not purchased Vendor Proof, that query does not return them. They are not penalised — they simply do not exist for that buyer.What It DoesCreates a VerifyRecord with lifecycleStatus = ACTIVE in the databaseSets complianceScore = 30, activating the baseline procurement trust signalEnables the verified badge on the vendor’s public Booppa profileMakes the vendor appear in procurement searches filtered by verified = trueGenerates an embeddable HTML badge for the vendor’s own website and proposalsActivates the Customer Activation Ladder at Level 1, which generates personalised upgrade recommendations on every subsequent dashboard loadWhy It Is The Foundation ProductWithout Vendor Proof, the confidenceScore starts at zero. The vendor does not appear in automated shortlists generated by the GeBIZ tender worker (Strategy 1), and is never notified when a buyer searches for suppliers in their sector (Strategy 6). Every other product on the platform builds on the trust baseline that Vendor Proof establishes.The purchase is permanent: one payment, no expiry, no renewal.2.  PDPA Snapshot  —  S$79  (one-time)The ProblemSingapore’s Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (amended 2021) imposes specific obligations on every organisation that handles personal data: documented consent procedures, DSAR response processes, data breach notification within three days, a nominated Data Protection Officer, and a published privacy notice. Penalties reach S$1 million.Most small and medium vendors are non-compliant without knowing it. They have privacy policies copied from templates, no documented breach procedures, and no DPO appointment. Large buyers — government-linked companies, statutory boards, and multinationals — are required to conduct due diligence on their suppliers’ data handling practices. A PDPA Snapshot from Booppa provides a structured, documented answer to that requirement.What It DoesLaunches the Booppa AI scanner against the vendor’s website and uploaded policy documentsEvaluates compliance across eight dimensions: data flow, consent management, DSAR procedures, data retention, breach notification, third-party processors, DPO appointment, and privacy noticeProduces a complianceScore with per-section breakdown (0–100 scale)Identifies specific violations with references to applicable PDPA legislation and penaltiesGenerates a professional A4 PDF report with score, gap analysis, immediate action recommendations, and optional Page 2 of detailed findingsUpdates the vendor’s confidenceScore, improving their position in procurement shortlistsBuyer ValueA vendor with a high PDPA risk level is classified as a contractual liability. The Snapshot report provides documented evidence of due diligence — a defensible record for the buyer’s procurement audit trail.3.  Notarization  —  S$69  (per document)The ProblemVendors routinely submit documents in procurement processes — ISO certifications, ACRA registration, professional licences, financial statements. Buyers have no reliable way to verify that these documents are authentic and have not been altered since the date stated on them. Traditional notarization is slow and expensive. Email attachments carry no integrity guarantee.What It DoesComputes a SHA-256 cryptographic hash of the uploaded file at the moment of submissionRecords the hash in the Booppa notarization ledger with a precise timestampGenerates a certificate PDF containing the hash and a public QR verification linkAny person — buyer, procurement officer, external auditor — can scan the QR code and confirm that the document has not been modified since notarizationWhy It Matters for Platform DepthEach notarized document increases the vendor’s evidenceCount and contributes to verificationDepth progression. Moving from STANDARD to DEEP verification requires five or more notarized documents. DEEP status activates the ELEVATED tier in the platform’s Dual Silent Standard ranking system, which increases the vendor’s weight in procurement ordering. Notarization is the mechanism that converts document volume into platform authority.4.  RFP Express  —  S$249  (one-time per RFP)The ProblemWhen a vendor responds to a GeBIZ tender or enterprise RFP, they submit a proposal against dozens or hundreds of competing bids. Without structured credibility documentation, the proposal is evaluated on price and narrative alone. Procurement officers cannot efficiently assess supplier risk, compliance posture, or tender readiness from an unstructured document.What It DoesVendor enters the RFP title, deadline, and sectorStripe processes the S$249 payment; the webhook fulfils within two secondsAn EvidencePackage record is created with tier = EXPRESSThe Booppa AI generates a Tender Readiness Score (0–100) based on the vendor’s current profileA professional A4 PDF is produced containing the readiness score, a structured evidence checklist with status per document, and a compliance summaryThe PDF is uploaded to secure cloud storage and delivered via email within seconds of paymentThe Network Effect — Strategy 6Immediately after fulfilment, the platform notifies the top five verified vendors in the same sector with an email stating that a procurement officer has submitted a requirement in their sector and they have been shortlisted. Buyer identity is never disclosed — the notification is fully PDPC-compliant.This transforms every RFP Express purchase from an isolated transaction into a multiplier: one buyer-side purchase generates up to five vendor-side upsell opportunities toward Vendor Proof. The mechanism is automatic, non-blocking, and fires only for RFP Express — not RFP Complete.AutoActivation GateEvery EvidencePackage with status = READY increments the platform’s RFP counter. At 100 purchases the platform automatically unlocks Phase 2: programmatic SEO pages, vendor comparison engine, and shortlist display. RFP Express is the primary driver of this gate.5.  RFP Complete  —  S$599  (one-time per RFP)The ProblemThe same problem as RFP Express, at a higher value tier. High-value tenders — infrastructure, large IT programmes, multi-year service contracts — require a more comprehensive credibility package. The vendor needs to demonstrate not just readiness for one RFP, but sustained procurement capability across multiple sectors and evaluation cycles.What It DoesRFP Complete follows the identical technical pipeline as RFP Express: Stripe payment, webhook fulfilment, PDF generation, secure upload, email delivery. The key differences are:The EvidencePackage is created with tier = COMPLETEThe generated PDF is titled “RFP Complete Dossier” and positions the vendor for enterprise-tier procurement visibilityStrategy 6 does not fire: this product is designed as a vendor-side credibility tool, not a lead generation mechanismBoth RFP Express and RFP Complete count equally toward the AutoActivation RFP gatePositioningWhere RFP Express is the entry point for active tender participation, RFP Complete signals sustained procurement maturity. The S$350 price gap reflects the difference in tender value being pursued — vendors spending S$599 on a credibility package are typically bidding for contracts in the S$100,000–S$1,000,000 range.6.  Tender Win Probability Calculator  —  FreeThe ProblemA vendor considering RFP Express or RFP Complete faces an abstract value proposition: “pay S$249 for a PDF”. The actual value — a measurable increase in procurement win probability against a specific tender — is invisible without quantification. Without a concrete number, the purchase decision relies on intuition rather than evidence.What It DoesThe vendor enters a GeBIZ tender number at booppa.com/tender-checkThe platform retrieves the tender’s sector from the existing TenderShortlist databaseIf the vendor is authenticated, the calculator reads their real profile from VendorStatusSnapshot: confidenceScore, verificationDepth, sectorPercentile, and evidenceCountA four-factor formula computes the current estimated win probability: BASE_RATE × PROFILE_MULTIPLIER × SECTOR_MULTIPLIER × EVIDENCE_MULTIPLIER, clamped between 5% and 85%The platform then projects what the probability would be with RFP Express and with RFP Complete, based on the CAL product targetsThe result shows the gap in percentage points — for example: current 19%, with RFP Express 48%, with RFP Complete 67%Human-readable gap reasons explain exactly what is pulling the score downWhy It Works as a Conversion MechanismThe calculator does not sell a PDF. It sells the gap between 19% and 67%. A vendor bidding on a S$200,000 contract sees that closing the gap costs S$249 or S$599. The expected value calculation is immediate and concrete. No other conversion mechanism in the platform is as direct.The tool is fully functional without any new database tables, environment variables, or external dependencies. It reads exclusively from data the platform already collects.7 – 8.
